Forensics Workshop


This Saturday, Cyber# is hosting a forensics workshop! No, we won't be collecting fingerprints and hair samples.

Forensics challenges are a type of CTF (capture the flag) challenges that require you to find hidden information in a static data file. So yes, you will get to play detective as we analyze different types of files and decode the information they contain. All majors and levels of experience are welcome! You can sign up for the workshop here.

When: Saturday, Feb 29, 1-2 pm

Where: Unix Lab, C2
