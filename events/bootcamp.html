Cyber# Bootcamp


Want to learn hacking but have no idea where to start?

We’ve got you! Cyber# is hosting a boot camp for its first event of the semester!

Over the weekend, we'll be holding a series of workshops that introduce beginners to the basics of solving Capture the Flag (CTF) challenges. These challenges are a great (and fun) way to develop hacking skills!

So if you don’t have any plans for Valentine’s Day, bring a friend and sign up for the event here.

When: Friday, Feb 14, 11 am - 3 pm

And Saturday, Feb 15, 11 am - 3 pm

Where: Unix Lab (C2- 3rd floor beside library)

Lunch will be served. Beginners are highly encouraged to join!
